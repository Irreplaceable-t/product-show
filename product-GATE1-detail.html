Product Introduction

Our Non Rising Stem (NRS) Gate Valve is designed for on-off control of fluid flow in industrial pipelines. The non-rising stem design means the stem does not move vertically when the valve is operated, making it ideal for applications with limited vertical space.

Key Features

  • Non-rising stem design saves installation space
  • Full port design for minimal pressure drop
  • Bi-directional sealing for reliable shut-off
  • Corrosion-resistant materials for long service life
  • Easy maintenance and operation
  • Compliant with international standards

Applications

Our NRS Gate Valves are widely used in:

  • Water and wastewater treatment plants
  • Oil and gas pipelines
  • Chemical processing plants
  • Power generation facilities
  • Mining operations
  • Pulp and paper industry
