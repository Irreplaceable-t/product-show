About Our Company

Cenhix - Your certified partner for safe and professional services.

Founded in 2010, GlobalTrade Co., Ltd is a professional foreign trade company integrating product development, production and export. Headquartered in Shanghai, we have established long-term and stable cooperative relations with more than 50 manufacturers and suppliers in China, and our products are exported to more than 80 countries and regions in Europe, America, Southeast Asia, the Middle East and Africa.

Our core business covers industrial products, daily consumer goods, electronic products, textile products and other categories. We adhere to the business philosophy of "quality first, customer foremost", and provide one-stop foreign trade solutions for global customers, including product sourcing, customization, quality inspection, logistics and customs clearance.
